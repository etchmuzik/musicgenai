‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate my first track?

1. Open MusicGen AI and sign in
2. Tap "Create" and describe your music
3. Choose genre, mood, and duration
4. Tap "Generate" and wait for AI magic!

What's included in the free plan?

Free users get 10 points and can generate up to 3 tracks per day. Each track costs 1-3 points depending on length and complexity.

Can I use generated music commercially?

Yes! All music you generate with MusicGen AI is yours to use for personal and commercial projects without additional licensing.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí MusicGen AI ‚Üí Cancel Subscription. You can also manage subscriptions from the App Store.

Why is my music generation taking a long time?

AI music generation typically takes 30-90 seconds. Longer tracks and complex prompts may take up to 2 minutes. Check your internet connection if it's taking longer.

Can I extend or remix existing tracks?

Yes! Pro and Unlimited subscribers can use our Extend and Remix features to transform any track in your library.

How do I share my music?

Tap any track in your library, then tap "Share". You can share publicly in the community, send to friends, or export to other apps.

What audio formats are supported?

All tracks are generated in high-quality MP3 format (320kbps). Pro subscribers can also download in WAV format for professional use.
