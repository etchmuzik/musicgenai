Frequently Asked Questions

How do credits work?
Each song generation uses 5 credits. Credits refresh monthly on your billing date. Unused credits don't roll over to the next month. Remix and extend features use 3 credits each.
Can I change plans anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
What AI models are available?
We offer multiple AI models including Suno (v3, v3.5, v4, v4.5), Udio, DiffRhythm, Ace Step, and Mmaudio. Premium plans get access to the latest and most advanced models first.
Do I own the music I create?
Yes! With Pro and Unlimited plans, you own full commercial rights to all music you generate. Free and Starter plans include personal use rights only.
Is there a free trial?
We offer a generous free plan with 10 credits per month so you can try the platform. Additionally, new users get 50 bonus credits on signup to explore all features.
